Who We Are


Liuzzi Property Group is a diverse family organisation founded in the 1950s by the patriarch Fred Liuzzi. The business spans across multiple industries including property, hospitality, investment and manufacturing.

Built on a foundation of integrity, expertise and business acumen, the group consolidate their knowledge to manage and grow the organisation for future generations.

Supported by a professional team of likeminded individuals Liuzzi Property Group has continued to grow into a value driven and progressive organisation. 

What We Do


With a wealth of experience spanning two generations, Liuzzi Property Group manages a diverse range of enterprises. Our dedicated team provides a unique skill set allowing us to manage all aspects of the ownership cycle.

  • Asset Management

    Our team is highly qualified with demonstrated skills and experience to focus and deliver on investment performance.

  • Property Management

    The group provides comprehensive solutions in all phases of a property lifecycle, whether it be development, construction or investment.

  • Development

    We’re highly committed to sourcing value-add opportunities to implement development and repositioning strategies.
